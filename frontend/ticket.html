Help
How to book a ticket?
Select a service like Train, Bus, Airplane, Metro, Cab or Other. Enter From city, To city, travel date, number of passengers, choose class, select payment method, enter PIN and tap Pay & Book Ticket.
Why is payment not working?
Check whether all fields are filled, From and To are different, and your entered PIN matches your login password stored in session.
Where are my booked tickets stored?
Every successful booking is saved in Ticket History using your browser local storage. It stays there even after refresh until you delete it manually.
Can I delete a receipt?
Yes. Open Ticket History and tap Delete on any receipt. You can also remove everything using Delete All History.
What details are saved in the receipt?
Travel type, route, date, passengers, class, payment method, amount paid, booking ID, booked time and booked user name.
Security note
This demo page stores history only in browser local storage. If browser data is cleared manually, the saved receipts will also be removed.
